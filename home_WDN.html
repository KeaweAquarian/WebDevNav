Australia

Australia Zodiacs take you up the spectacular King George River, where you’ll feel dwarfed by the towering red rock canyon walls, then cooled by the mist of King George Falls. View an outdoor Aboriginal art gallery, portraying images of the Wandjina spirits. Explore the Ord River, one of the most stunning river systems in Australia. Visit Montgomery Reef, where the tide drops so rapidly that waters trapped atop the reef create a raging torrent as they escape.

Greece

Greece is synonymous for far too many with ‘sun, islands, sea’. Don’t ignore the sun-kissed archipelagos and 15,000km of coast but discover its panoply of other highlights. Glorious mountains mix stark peaks with lush alpine meadows and woods – plus dazzling snow fields in winter. There are rivers and dramatic gorges, plus quiet hiking trails as easy or difficult as you wish. Everywhere you'll find richly diverse flora and fauna. Thousands of years of colourful history place a host of legendary gods and humans amid one of the world’s finest concentrations of stupendous archaeological memories. Enjoy the sun – but explore.

Caribbean

The Caribbean is a joyous mosaic of islands beckoning paradise-hunters, an explosion of color, fringed by beaches and soaked in rum. It’s a lively and intoxicating profusion of people and places spread over 7000 islands (fewer than 10% are inhabited). But, for all they share, there’s also much that makes them different.

Planning Assistance

Before any trip make sure to review these checklists!

  1. Traveling overseas
    1. Essentials
      1. Your passport!
      2. Cash and debit card on hand
      3. Sunscreen and bug spray
      4. Headphones, chargers, and adaptors
    2. Recommended
      1. Travel-sized toiletries
      2. Any medications or vitamins you need
      3. Earplugs and eye mask
  2. Just for fun!
    1. Reusable Water Bottle
    2. Books
    3. Camera
  1. Traveling Domestic
    1. Essentials
      1. Comfortable Walking Shoes
      2. drivers licence
      3. Clothing and Shoes
      4. Sun Protection
    2. Recommended
      1. Student ID card
      2. A Few Plastic Bags
      3. Rain Jacket
  2. Just for fun!
    1. One or Two Nice Outfits
    2. Hiking Boots
    3. Luggage Locks
